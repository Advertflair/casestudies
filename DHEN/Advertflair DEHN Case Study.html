Case Study: DEHN India

Turning Trade Show Foot Traffic into Qualified Leads

How DEHN India leveraged an interactive 3D product experience to revolutionize expo engagement and supercharge their lead generation.

The Challenge: Breaking Through the Noise

DEHN India invested heavily in expos, but their traditional booth setup with static posters and brochures wasn't capturing attention. Attendees walked by without a second glance.

Static Displays

Posters and brochures failed to invite exploration or showcase the complexity of their products effectively.

Overwhelmed Sales Teams

In high-footfall conditions, the sales team couldn't scale to provide in-depth demos to every interested visitor.

Complex Value Proposition

Visitors struggled to understand the unique value and technical advantages of DEHN's products in under 30 seconds.

Lack of Digital Engagement

No modern, digital layer to engage a younger, mobile-first audience expecting interactive experiences.

The Solution: An Interactive 3D WebApp

We designed a fully interactive 3D web application for kiosks that showcased DEHN's entire product range in stunning, real-time detail. This transformed their booth into a must-see destination.

BEFORE
AFTER

360° Product Viewer

Visitors could rotate, zoom, and explore product internals, demonstrating complexity without words.

Click-to-Learn Features

Tapping on product sections revealed feature highlights and short, embedded videos that explained functionality instantly.

Tailored for Kiosks

A touch-first interface and an auto-looping idle mode ensured a smooth, seamless user experience with no app downloads needed.

The Impact: Measurable Success

The results from the very first expo were immediate and dramatic, providing a clear return on investment and transforming their event strategy.

3x

Longer Booth Visits

Visitors were captivated, staying for full walkthroughs and creating more "wow" moments.

60%

Faster Product Demos

Demos were cut from 10 minutes to 4, increasing booth coverage for each sales rep.

2x

More Product Inquiries

Leads collected were more informed and qualified, with 40% asking to share the demo post-event.
