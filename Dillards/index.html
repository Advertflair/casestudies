The Problem: Traditional Photo Agency Limitations

Dillards, one of America's largest fashion retailers, faced the classic e-commerce photography dilemma: balancing quality, speed, and cost across thousands of product launches.

Expensive Photo Shoots

Traditional photo agencies required costly studio setups, professional models, makeup artists, and extensive post-production for every product variation.

Slow Time-to-Market

Product launches delayed by weeks waiting for photo shoots, model availability, and post-production—critical time lost in fast-fashion cycles.

Limited Creative Control

Once photos were taken, making changes required entirely new shoots. No flexibility for different poses, backgrounds, or styling variations.

Inconsistent Accessory Pairing

The biggest industry challenge: AI solutions couldn't properly pair accessories with garments, leading to unrealistic or mismatched combinations.

Our Solution: Enterprise AI Platforms

We created custom Glam AI and Style AI platforms that combine cutting-edge generative AI with human expertise, delivering photo-agency quality at revolutionary speed and cost through controlled AI workflows.
