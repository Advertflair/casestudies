The Challenge: Breaking Through Amazon's Noise

British Biologicals had strong brand recognition and trusted products, but their Amazon listings weren't converting browsers into buyers. Plain packshots couldn't compete in a marketplace where parents need to see products in real family contexts.

Sterile Product Images

Basic packshots on white backgrounds failed to show how products fit into real family moments and daily routines.

Missing Emotional Connection

Parents couldn't visualize their children enjoying the products, creating a barrier to purchase decisions.

Low Amazon Performance

Click-through rates and conversions were below industry benchmarks despite the quality of their nutritional products.

Our Solution: Lifestyle Photo Renders

We transformed British Biologicals' product presentations with photorealistic 3D lifestyle renders that showcase products in authentic family settings, creating emotional connections that drive purchases.
